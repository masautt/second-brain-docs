# pinapp

pinapp is the people domain of Second Brain: contacts, relationships, life events, and family-tree visualization.

## Goals

- Centralize people and relationship data in a queryable model
- Visualize family and social connections
- Preserve context tags across work/family/friends/networking

## Components

| Component | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| `pinapp-web` (planned) | Future host app for people-domain routes |
| `pinapp-ftree-comp` | Family tree canvas and people details |

## Data Domains

Primary schemas: `people`, `property`
